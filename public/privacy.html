Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

GoStar Digital LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates the GoStar Digital platform at gostar.digital. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use GoStar Digital, we may collect:

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Organization details (business name, location, description)
  • Team registration data (team name, contact info, player count)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the GoStar Digital platform
  • Enable event creation and team registration
  • Communicate with you about your account and events
  • Improve our platform and develop new features

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help operate our platform (hosting, analytics). Organizer information is visible on their public pages by design.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure hosting to protect your data.

Children

GoStar Digital is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at hello@gostar.digital
